Let's Begin

Easy Stethoscope Assembly



1. Gather supplies:

a. Small plastic cup (top of medicine bottle)
b. Saran wrap
c. Rubber band
d. Plastic straw
e. Wire headphones (with microphone attachment)

2. Cut a hole the size of the straw at bottom of cup

3. Stick small amount of the straw through the hole and adhere to cup with tape

4. Cover the mouth of the cup with Saran wrap and stretch as taut as possible. Fasten to cup with rubber band

5. Push microphone attachment into straw at the end furthest from the cup. Wrap in insulating material (cotton, paper towel, etc)

1Now, let’s start breathing!


*Please ensure you have your microphone enabled on the browser*

1. Click the "Breathe" button below to start analyzing
2. Place the stethoscope on chest and breathe
3. Breathe in and out at a normal rate for 30 seconds into your microphone/ stethoscope.
4. Say your name: “My name is Caroline”
5. Click "Stop" and download the recording for your own record and/or send it to your doctor.
